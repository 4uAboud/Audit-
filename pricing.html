Frequently asked
Do credits expire?▾
No. Credits never expire. Buy when you need them and use them at your own pace — whether that's one audit a week or ten in a single day.
What counts as one audit?▾
One audit = one full analysis run. You can upload multiple screenshots in a single run — they all count as one audit. Re-running or adjusting the configuration and running again uses another credit.
Can I share credits with my team?▾
Team sharing is available on the Agency pack — up to 5 team members can draw from a shared credit pool. Starter and Professional packs are for individual use.
What payment methods are accepted?▾
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported at checkout.
Can I get a refund?▾
Yes — if you've used fewer than 3 credits from a new pack, contact us within 7 days for a full refund. We want you to be satisfied with every audit.
Do you offer custom enterprise plans?▾
Yes. For teams needing 500+ audits per month, custom branding, SSO, or API access, contact us for a custom quote. We offer volume pricing and dedicated support for enterprise customers.
